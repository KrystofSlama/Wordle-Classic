Privacy Policy

Effective Date: April 9, 2025

Wordle Classic! does not collect, store, or share any personal data from users by default.

The only time any personal information is collected is when a user chooses to submit feedback in the “Want to Help?” section of the app. In that case, the following information may be collected:

  • Name (if provided)
  • Email address (if provided)

This information is used solely to receive suggestions, respond to feedback, or improve the app experience. It is not shared with third parties or used for any marketing purposes.

No analytics, advertising, or tracking services are used in this app at this time.
